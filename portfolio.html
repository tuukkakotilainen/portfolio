CSIT identity management self-service portal


This project was part of Digiprojekti -course where we had CSIT oy as employer. They were renovating their identity management service and we students provided them ideas through our work. Our task was to make self-service portal where users could easily check, modify or delete their personal information. This project was made in a group of four and my area of resposibility was frontend coding. Languages used in this project are JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS. The website was never published but here is link to the Github repository: https://github.com/tuukkakotilainen/digiprojekti

Green Trip


This work was part of course that focused on prototyping. We made a prototype of mobile app in a group of three. The idea of the app is to make it easier to choose ecological ways to travell by showing every options carbon footprint. Users are also encouraged to choose eco friendly options by level system and ranking. The prototype was made with Axure RP 9. You can take take a closer look at the prototype via this link: https://aj53td.axshare.com The prototype has Finnish and English versions available through the same link.
